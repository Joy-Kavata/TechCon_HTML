History

TechCon was founded in 2004 at Nairobi, Kenya. At this time it only had 20 member but now it has over 100,000 mambers from different parts of Africa. Its head quarters is in Nairobi, with other hubs inside and ouside Kenya. It is an African based organisation.

Mission

Our goal is to make Africa a better place to live in by incorporating technology in real life. Our core principles include resilience, determination ,team work and being open minded.
